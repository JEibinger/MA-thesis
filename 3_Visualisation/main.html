The CoReMA project is putting an interdisciplinary focus on the cross-cultural research of medieval cooking recipes and their interrelation.

Data Visualisation in the humanities

Data visualisation is useful mainly in two ways:

  • Studying the data in a way more suited for the human mind and understanding, e.g. visual input vs. data in rows and columns.
  • Presenting findings and observations to a third party.
